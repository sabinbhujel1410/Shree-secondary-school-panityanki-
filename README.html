About Our School

Welcome to Shree Siddhababa Secondary School

Shree Siddhababa Secondary School was established on Jestha 24, 2046 BS (1990 AD) by local visionary Mr. Binod Thapa and community members. It is one of the oldest and most respected community schools in Sindhuli district.

We provide quality education from ECD to Grade 12 with both English and Nepali medium classes. The school has consistently delivered excellent SEE results and is known for its disciplined environment, dedicated teachers, and modern facilities.

Principal: Dr. Hari Prasad Pokhrel
Students: Approximately 600+
Staff: 36 qualified teachers and support staff

Academic Programs

  • ECD & Basic Level (1–8) – Nepali & English Medium
  • Secondary Level (9–10) – Compulsory subjects + Optional (Computer, Account etc.)
  • Grade 11–12 (Management & Computer Engineering)

We focus on holistic development with special emphasis on STEM education, moral values, and practical skills. Scholarships are provided to talented, hardworking, and economically disadvantaged students.
